# Personas

## Booth Rep ("Riley")

- On their feet at the booth for 6–10 hours/day at a trade show.
- Holds the phone in one hand, sometimes a drink or business cards in the other.
- Needs to capture leads in 5–15 seconds without breaking eye contact.
- Spotty Wi-Fi, weak cellular signal in convention halls is normal.
- Doesn't want to type. Wants to talk.
- Knows the product, knows what qualifies a lead.

## Booth Lead / Show Owner ("Sam")

- Same as Riley but also responsible for the show as a whole.
- Wants to see, mid-show, what's been captured and by whom.
- Sets up the show in the app before day 1 (paste iCapture CSV header, invite reps).
- Exports CSV at end-of-day or end-of-show.

## App Admin ("Alex")

- Internal IT / tech lead. Manages provider API keys.
- Decides which AI providers to use; runs A/B tests.
- Investigates failed captures via the processing-jobs UI.
- Rotates KEK and provider keys per security policy.

## Post-Show Ops ("Pat")

- Receives the exported CSV after the show.
- Uploads to iCapture Lead Upload Tool.
- Doesn't use our app during the show — only sees its output.
- Cares about CSV format matching iCapture's expectations exactly.
